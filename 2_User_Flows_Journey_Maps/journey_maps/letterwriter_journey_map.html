Key Persona Differences & Insights

Access Preferences

  • Faculty consider both guest/login options
  • Admins always choose guest (no credentials)
  • Clinical users avoid accounts completely

Upload Behaviors

  • Faculty use file picker (familiar)
  • Admins prefer drag-and-drop (efficiency)
  • Clinical users need browse button fallback

Pain Point Patterns

  • Faculty: Time pressure from other duties
  • Admins: Bulk processing limitations
  • Clinical: Technology intimidation and forgetting process

Success Factors

  • Faculty: Clear tracking and account benefits
  • Admins: Speed and batch processing capability
  • Clinical: Simple, error-free one-time experience
