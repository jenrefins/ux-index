Key Design Implications

Progressive Enhancement

Support both basic (email/SMS) and advanced (app-based) authentication methods to serve all user comfort levels.

Clear Communication

Provide upfront explanations, progress indicators, and contextual help to reduce anxiety and confusion.

Flexible Verification

Offer email and SMS options with easy switching to accommodate different user preferences and reliability needs.

Graceful Recovery

Design robust error handling and recovery flows for users who face issues or long gaps between usage.
